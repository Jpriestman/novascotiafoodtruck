WHO ARE WE

The Nova Scotia Food Truck Association has a wide variety of food trucks in Halifax and across Nova Scotia. Our goal is to support food truck owners and to act as a singular point of contact for the public looking to contact or hire food trucks near them.
We offer catering and location management services to make it easier for the public to take advantage of food truck services in Halifax and across Nova Scotia. We also help food truck vendors understand zoning codes, ordinances, and procedures so they can better understand the industry.

FOOD TRUCK CATERING OPTIONS

Our collection of food trucks offer many food truck catering options. From corporate catering, to food trucks at weddings and festivals, or find the nearest curbside food truck to grab a fresh lunch nearby.
